## For In-Person Testing (Bar/Event)

**Script:**

"Hey! We're testing a new app that gets you and your car home safely. Want to try it? It's free during beta. Takes 2 minutes to sign up, then you can request a driver. If anything breaks, just let me know!"

**Key Points:**
- Free during beta
- Quick signup
- Real-time feedback appreciated
- Screenshots help if something breaks

## Tester Checklist

Send this to testers after they sign up:

- [ ] Can you sign up successfully?
- [ ] Can you add a vehicle?
- [ ] Can you request a driver?
- [ ] Does the status show "Looking for driver"?
- [ ] Can a driver accept your request?
- [ ] Does status update when driver accepts?
- [ ] Can you complete the trip?
- [ ] Does the feedback form work?
- [ ] Did you answer "Would you use this again?"

## What to Track

**Metrics to watch:**
1. Signup completion rate
2. Vehicle add success rate
3. Request submission success
4. Driver acceptance rate
5. Trip completion rate
6. Feedback submission rate
7. "Would use again" % (target: >70%)

**Questions to ask:**
- "What was confusing?"
- "What would make you use this again?"
- "What's missing?"
- "Would you pay for this? How much?"

**Where to find testers:**
